Writing

I think a lot about how we become competent and what it means to be human — experiences, science, economics, morals, religion — understood in terms of skills.

I'm influenced by Christopher Alexander, Michael Polanyi, Charles Taylor, Matthew Crawford, Ralph Waldo Emerson, Michael Pollon, Thich Nhat Hanh, and C.S. Lewis.

I have two ongoing writing projects:

Gleanings — personal reflections, small essays, and falsework for my research projects.

Skillful Notes — research on competence, skill, and human capability.
