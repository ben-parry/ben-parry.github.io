In 2021, the Tims Hortons app was one of the top 20 most used apps and the #1 Food and Drink app in Canada ahead of competitors like Uber Eats, DoorDash and Skip the Dishes. We have over 4000 restaurants across the country -meaning there is a Tims for every 10,000 Canadians - and the vast majority of Canadians visit one of these locations every single month.

If you’re Canadian you can probably remember a role that Tims has played in your life whether as a first job, an after practice donut or a haven on a long-road trip.

Given our brand and scale, we believe we have an opportunity to create a platform to offer services to Canadians that extends well beyond our existing restaurant base. Our vision is to provide Canada with a digital ecosystem that delivers evolving forms of convenience, value, connection, and delight every day.

We’ve already started with in-app games like the NHL Hockey Challenge that has hundreds of thousands of daily active users but we have much further to go.

To achieve these ambitions we know we need to build a best-in-class product, design, and engineering organisation. We aren’t historically a tech company. We got where we are by making the best coffee and donuts and getting them to people fast. Not by writing code.

Transformations like this are hard. There are few examples of traditional companies that have developed great tech capabilities but it does happen. We think it’s a worthwhile challenge and we have full support from our company’s senior leadership to make the changes and create the needed culture.

We’re now looking for the first hires to form the foundation of a team that can make this a reality across engineering, product and design.

If you are excited by building a rigorous, creative and effective engineering organisation and want to work at Canada’s most loved brand to make products that will be used by millions of Canadians then reach out.
